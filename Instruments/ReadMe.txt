*Instruments folder*

Into this folder you can drop all your instrument (soundbank) alias files from any of the Virtual Instrument applications that you wish to open from within the Opusmodus scores, which are part of your instrument setup.

Example:

Vienna Ensamble Pro:
(load-instrument "Piano.viframe64")
(load-instrument "String Quartet.viframe64")

Kontakt:
(load-instrument "All Percussion.nki")

EastWest Play:
(load-instrument "Classical-orchestra.ewi")

Ableton Live:
(load-instruments "Electro-1.als")

Reaktor:
(load-instruments "ElectroAcoustic1.ens")
